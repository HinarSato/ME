Main Goals 🌟

  • Create a sustainable housing model for the UAE.
  • Use renewable energy and reduce environmental impact.
  • Inspire youth and communities to adopt green solutions.
  • Demonstrate that students can drive innovation.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Target Audience

Ministry guests, parents, and eco-curious minds of all ages.

🎯 Additional Goals

  • Promote hands-on learning in green architecture.
  • Bridge education and environmental responsibility.
  • Introduce the concept of eco-communities in the UAE.

Eco Features 🏡

Solar Power: Off-grid and fully solar-powered. ☀️

Water Recycling: Rainwater harvesting and greywater reuse. 💧

Natural Cooling: Passive cooling with thick walls and green roofs. ❄️

Green Roof: Trees, plants, and an eco-friendly pool. 🌿🏊
